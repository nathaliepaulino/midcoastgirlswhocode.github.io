Raisin Drop Cookies

Ingredients:


1 cup sugar
1 tbsp. shortening
1 3/4 cup flour
1 tsp. vanilla
1/4 cup milk
1 cup raisins
3 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. cream of tartar
1 egg

Instructions

Mix sugar and shortening add beaten egg and milk. Add sifted dry ingredients. Add vanilla and raisins. Raisins may be left out. More flour may be needed to make a stiff dough. Cook in moderate oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
