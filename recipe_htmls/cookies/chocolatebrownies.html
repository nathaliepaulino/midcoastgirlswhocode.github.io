Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:


2 squares unsweetened chocolate
1/2 cup butter
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions

Melt chocolate over low heat. Cream together butter and sugar. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in chocolate and then flour. Fold in nuts. Pour into greased 9-inch square pan. Bake in 325ยบ F oven for 25 minutes. Cool slightly. Cut into bars. Makes about 24.
