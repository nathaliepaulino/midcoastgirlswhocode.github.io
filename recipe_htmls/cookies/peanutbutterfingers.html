Peanut Butter Fingers

Ingredients:


1 cup packed brown sugar
1 cup all purpose flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp.salt
1/2 cup butter
1/3 cup peanut butter
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 egg
1 cup oats
1 package semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Combine brown sugar, flour, soda and salt. Cut in butter and peanut butter. Stir in egg, vanilla, and oats. Press mixture into a 9x13-inch pan. Bake in 350ยบ F oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Remove from oven. Sprinkle immediately with chocolate pieces. After 5 minutes, spread chocolate evenly over baked dough. Drizzle topping over chocolate. Cool.Cut into small bars. Makes about 48.
