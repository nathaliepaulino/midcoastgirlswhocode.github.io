Rice Crispie Treats

This recipe is so simple, yet timeless and delicious. Feel free to get creative with additions or changes. For example, you can use lots of different kinds of cereals, and you can add different things for flavor such as 1/4 cup of peanut butter. You can also add food coloring or sprinkles to make your treat the life of the party!

Ingredients


40 Marshmallows (about 4 cups)
1/4 cup margarine
6 cups Rice Crispies

Instructions

1) Melt marchmallows with margarine in large saucepan over low heat, stirring until completely melted.
2) Add rice crispies, stir until well coated
3) Using a greased spatula or waxed paper, press mixture evenly into greased 13x9 pan and let cool.
4) Cut into squares when cool.
